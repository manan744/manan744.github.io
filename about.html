About Us

The Glassmic is the new venture of Saraswati Glass Industries, a name that has been serving the glass industry with excellence since 1960. Rooted in Firozabad, India’s renowned glass manufacturing hub, our legacy is built on decades of craftsmanship, trust, and innovation in glass handicraft manufacturing.

The Glassmic has been launched to bring this rich heritage into a modern, design-driven gifting and home décor brand. We offer complete gifting solutions, including corporate gifting, wedding gifting, festive gifting, and premium home décor.

With in-house manufacturing, skilled artisans, and a deep understanding of glass artistry, we ensure superior quality, customization, and timely delivery. Every product reflects our commitment to elegance, durability, and timeless design.
